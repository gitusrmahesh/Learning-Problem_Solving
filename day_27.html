Day 27: Mixed Problem Practice (Arrays + Strings)

Today's Focus Concepts

Today's problems are designed to strengthen your ability to manipulate and analyze data structures that combine arrays and strings. We will focus on techniques like hashing for quick lookups, in-place array modifications, custom sorting logic, and careful string parsing. This session bridges the gap between basic data structure knowledge and complex real-world problem-solving.
