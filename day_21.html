Key Bitwise Operators

  • & (AND): Sets bit to 1 if both bits are 1.
  • | (OR): Sets bit to 1 if one of two bits is 1.
  • ^ (XOR): Sets bit to 1 if bits are different.
  • << : Left shifts bits (multiply by 2).
  • >> : Right shifts bits (divide by 2).
